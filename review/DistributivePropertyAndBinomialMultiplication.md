Distributive property and binomial multiplication example:

$$ 3x (x+4) (x+1) (x-2) $$

$$ (3x^2 + 12x) (x+1) (x-2) $$

$$ (3x^2 + 12x) (x+1) (x-2) $$

$$ 3x^3 + 3x^2 + 12x^2 + 12x) (x-2) $$

$$ (3x^3 + 15x^2 + 12x) (x-2) $$

$$ 3x^4 - 6x^3 + 15x^3 + 12x^2 - 24x $$

Result after combining like terms:

$$ 3x^4 + 9x^3 - 18x^2 - 24x $$
